2 Days of Agile Reality

Agile Means Business

This is a two-day Agile Workshop for the Agile-integrated enterprise. We cover four subjects for you:
  1. Agile for HR
  2. Agile for Management
  3. Agile for Organization
  4. Agile for Clients

A Kickass Lecturing Team

We are experienced in sewage treatment. This is a no bullshit zone. Chris Mann and Pierre Neis are driven by meaning and are here to serve your market effectiveness. Pierre is a spearhead in developing theories applied to all pain points in a business and has been doctoring patients to health for over 20 years from banks to startups. Chris has achieved direct results from empirical systematic analysis. Chris and Pierre are proud to propose perfectly conflicting viewpoints that become complementary for you in our friendliness.

Fun

This seminar is about learning and labor. Reading in advance will be David Snowdens' articles, Reinventing Organizations, etc. We hope to distribute some of the know-how so that, more than give you a fast ride for two days, we will give you key insights. At the same time, we expect to be challenged and sharpen our own perspectives.

A Steal

For only 800 euros for the two days, you will be parachuted in one of the most renewing spaces in all of Paris to re-explore meaning in the workplace. We will bring you to this jewel in the heart of Paris to explore a jewel in the heart of your dedication to what you already love in your job. 

Content for Performance

Yes, we are driven by our complementary abstract ideas, but only because they can give persuasive solutions to your fuck-ups and lag-behinds in business performance. When you come to this seminar, you will come with the latest in theory and we will engage you in your practice. 
Agile used to be good when it was everybody else. Now, we want Agile to be everybody. How can you want to act one way, and everyone else in your enterprise to act in another way? Huh? As a matter of fact, we don't even need to call it Agile anymore. We can use intelligence to call it the way you decide to do things.
This content is designed to explore Agile theories applied to the integrated enterprise. We allow ourselves to think about how more closely related our different silos really are, and we allow ourselves to live them. Hence, you will leave this seminar with a challenged perspective, your own perspective, on the bold way forward for your enterprise.
